Instructions

Click start then use your directional keys (or the colored arrows on mobile) to turn your snake.
To pause or resume press your space bar (or the pause/play button on mobile).
Eat bugs (red squares) to increase your score and speed.
If you hit a wall or yourself, you lose.
You can restart the game by clicking the restart button.
Have fun!
